ADVANCED INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND MANAGEMENT (AITM) is a pioneer engineering college in the Palwal District, set up in the year 2006. AITM believes in imparting futuristic technical education & instills a high pattern of discipline through dedicated & trained faculty, who shall set global standards, making the students technologically superior, ethically strong with a renewed moral sensibility .




CAMPUS

The AEI campus is sited on the Delhi-Mathura National Highway-2 in Village Aurangabad, Tehsil Hodal in District Palwal, Haryana.The AEI campus provides the internet with 30 Mbps & more than 700 computer systems, an air-conditioned seminar hall with 220 seating capacity, separate library for each institution, laboratories, workshop, advanced ERP system, ATM, cafeteria, security, health care, conveyance, sports facility and separate hostel for boys & girls. The AEI campus conducts more than 12 academic programmes as graduate, post-graduate and diploma in various disciplines of engineering & technology, management, pharmacy, computer application and education field The campus 16 km away from the Town Hodal, 10 km from the Town Hatin and 13 km from the Palwal City. The campus is spread over in 25 acres of land on the outskirt of the Palwal City. There are four colleges established on the AEI campus as Advanced Institute of Technology and Management, Advanced College of Technology and Management, Advanced Institute of Pharmacy and Advanced Institute of Education.
